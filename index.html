OUR STORY

Rooted in Irish Tradition, Reimagined for Today

An Solas — 'The Light' in Irish Gaelic — is more than a restaurant. It is a celebration of Ireland's rich culinary heritage, guided by Chef Máire O'Connor's vision to honor the past while embracing innovation.

Nestled in the heart of Dublin, our dining room welcomes you with the warmth of an Irish hearth — candlelit tables, reclaimed wood, and the subtle melodies of traditional Irish music.
